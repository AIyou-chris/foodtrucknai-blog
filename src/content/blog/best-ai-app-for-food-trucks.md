## What AI Actually Does for a Food Truck

Skip the buzzwords. Here's the real list.

### Automated Upsells

This is the single highest-ROI feature.

When a customer orders a burger, the system automatically asks: "Want to add fries and a drink for $3.50?"

Half of them say yes.

You did nothing. The AI handled it. On 80 orders/day, at a 40% conversion and $3 per upsell — that's **$35,000+ per year in extra revenue.**

### Smarter Menu Timing

AI watches what's selling and when. It can help you know:
- Which items to push during slow periods
- What runs out fastest on Fridays
- When to 86 something before you run out and disappoint customers

Less waste. Less 86ing mid-service. Better margins.

### Demand Forecasting

Stop showing up to an event with too much food or not enough.

AI tools that track your order history can tell you what to prep based on the event type, day of week, and weather patterns.

Less food cost. Less end-of-night trash.

### Instant Analytics

At the end of a shift, do you actually know:
- What your best-selling item was?
- What your average ticket was?
- How many customers you served in the first hour vs. the last?

Most operators guess. AI gives you the answer in 3 seconds.

## The 3 Things to Look For

When evaluating an AI tool for your truck, ask:

1. **Does it make upsells automatic?** If not, you're leaving your biggest revenue lever on the table.
2. **Does it give me real-time data on my phone?** You need this mid-service, not after.
3. **Is it built for food trucks, not restaurants?** Restaurant software assumes a fixed location, a full staff, and a complex POS setup. Trucks need something lean.
